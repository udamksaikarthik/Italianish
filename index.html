Our Story

Family-run, fiercely passionate, fully Italian at heart.

For over seven years, Italianish has been a small, cosy corner of Ocean Road where Giovanni — better known as Gio — and Ele welcome you like family. Every plate is cooked with care, every guest is greeted by name when they return. It's the closest you'll get to Italy without leaving South Shields.

From hand-made empanadas and slow-cooked ragù to wood-fired pizza and house-made tiramisu, our menu blends authentic Italian classics with Spanish flavours we love.
